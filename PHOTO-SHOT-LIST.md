## General Photography Notes

- **Mood:** Luxury, refined, editorial. NOT generic real estate listing photos.
- **Color grading:** Warm tones, slight desaturation, rich blacks. Think luxury magazine editorial.
- **Resolution:** All photos should be at MINIMUM 2x the listed dimensions for retina displays (e.g., 800×1000 → shoot at 1600×2000+).
- **Format:** JPG or WebP. Optimize for web — aim for 100-300KB per image after compression.
- **Consistency:** All photos should feel like they belong to the same brand. Consistent color grading and style across all images.
