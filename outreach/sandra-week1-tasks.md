# Sandra — Week 1 Daily Task List
## Double My Rental Marketing Launch

### DAILY TASKS (Mon-Fri, 4 hours/day)

#### Facebook Groups (1.5 hrs)
Post in 5 groups per day. Use posts from `social/facebook-posts.md`. Rotate through these groups:
1. Real Estate Investing (50K+ members)
2. Airbnb Hosts Community
3. Short Term Rental Investors
4. Tax Strategy for High Earners
5. Passive Income & Real Estate
6. STR & Vacation Rental Owners
7. Real Estate Tax Benefits
8. Airbnb Superhosts Network
9. Real Estate Wealth Building
10. High Income Professionals & Investing

**Rules:**
- Post value-first content ONLY (no selling in the post)
- End with "DM me if you want more details" or "Link in comments"
- Reply to EVERY comment within 2 hours
- Like and engage with other posts in the group (5 per group)
- Rotate posts — never post the same content twice in the same group

#### Directory Submissions (30 min)
Submit doublemyrental.com to 10 directories per day:
- Yelp, BBB, Manta, Hotfrog, Cylex
- Yellow Pages, Superpages, DexKnows
- Real estate directories: BiggerPockets, Roofstock
- Local business directories for Chi Ta's markets

#### Reddit (30 min)
- Find 5 threads about Airbnb investing, STR, or tax strategy
- Leave value-add comments (NOT promotional)
- Build karma on r/realestateinvesting, r/airbnb_hosts, r/tax
- After 2 weeks of karma building, start posting

#### Quora (30 min)
- Answer 2 questions per day about:
  - "Is Airbnb investing worth it?"
  - "How to start a short-term rental business"
  - "Best tax strategies for high earners"
  - "Airbnb vs long-term rental"
- Include subtle mention of doublemyrental.com in your bio
- Write 200+ word answers with real value

#### Reporting (30 min)
End of day, send Slack message to #charles-sandra with:
- Links to all posts made today
- Screenshots of engagement (likes, comments)
- Any leads or DMs received
- Questions or blockers
