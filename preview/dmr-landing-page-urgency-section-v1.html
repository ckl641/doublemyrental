2026 Is Your Last Shot at 100% Bonus Depreciation

The tax code's most powerful deduction for rental property owners expires this year — and the clock doesn't reset.

Under current tax law, 2026 is the final year you can claim 100% bonus depreciation on a cost segregation study. Starting in 2027, that drops to 80% — then 60%, 40%, and 20% in the years that follow.


For a rental property owner with a $500K property, that phaseout could mean leaving $30,000–$50,000 in first-year deductions on the table.
