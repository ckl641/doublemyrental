## 15.2 Compliance & Legal

### Advertising Compliance

| Rule | Implementation |
|---|---|
| No guaranteed income claims | Use "potential," "up to," "based on actual client results" with disclaimers |
| Testimonials need disclaimers | Add "Results not typical. Individual results vary." to all testimonial content |
| Tax advice disclaimers | "This is not tax advice. Consult your CPA." on all tax-related content |
| Real estate disclaimers | Comply with state-specific real estate advertising laws |
| FTC endorsement guidelines | Disclose if any testimonial was incentivized |
| Email CAN-SPAM | Unsubscribe link on every email, physical address in footer |

### Platform-Specific Rules

| Platform | Risk | Rule |
|---|---|---|
| Meta (FB/IG) Ads | Account ban for misleading claims | No "get rich" language, no before/after income screenshots without disclaimer |
| Google Ads | Disapproved ads for financial claims | Use approved financial services language, link to compliant landing page |
| YouTube | Demonetization for misleading content | Disclaimers in description, no clickbait income thumbnails |
| LinkedIn | Account restriction for spam | Limit connection requests to 100/week, personalize all outreach |
| Email | Spam folder / blacklisting | Warm up domain, authenticate (SPF/DKIM/DMARC), clean list monthly |
